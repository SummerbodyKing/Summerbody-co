## HIGH-VALUE TRAPS (bake into teaching)
- Initiating has only 2 processes: Develop Project Charter and Identify Stakeholders (people forget the second).
- Closing has only 1: Close Project or Phase.
- Validate Scope is M&C (customer acceptance), not Executing. Control Quality (internal, correct) comes before
  Validate Scope (external, accepted).
- Most-forgotten Executing (both 6th-ed additions): Manage Project Knowledge, Implement Risk Responses.
- Every KA has a "Plan ___ Management" process EXCEPT Integration (Develop PM Plan) and Stakeholder
  (Plan Stakeholder Engagement).
- "Monitor" appears in three names: Monitor Communications, Monitor Risks, Monitor Stakeholder Engagement (all M&C).
- Perform Integrated Change Control: change requests are an INPUT, approved change requests are the OUTPUT.

## WRONG-ANSWER REINFORCEMENT LOOP (run on EVERY missed question, no shortcuts)
1. Name the underlying PM concept (principle, not surface topic).
2. Map it: knowledge area + process.
3. Give that process's tools/techniques, inputs, outputs (show where the answer lives in the flow).
4. Why the correct answer is correct AND why each distractor is wrong (name the trap each was planted for).
5. Teach the elimination pattern: the exact stem phrase/signal that points to the right answer.
6. Create 2-3 fresh questions on the SAME concept, worded differently.
7. Log the miss (see MISSED-QUESTION LOG below) so weak-area patterns surface.
