No diet. No counting. No restriction lists. The Quick Start Guide is a daily system, not a meal plan. The point is to crowd out the bad food, not to eliminate it through willpower.

Do I need a gym? +

No. I lost the first 70 lbs without one. I thought people that are "too-fat" didn't belong in a gym, so I built the system to work in your kitchen, your neighborhood, and your bedroom. The truth is, you don't need the gym. You need a community. The gym is optional. The community is mandatory.

How is this different from MyFitnessPal or Noom? +

Apps don't text you. Apps don't pick up the phone when you're about to eat a bag of chips. Apps don't lose 180 lbs themselves. I do all three.

You watch TV. You scroll Instagram. You drive somewhere. The 5 Pillars fit inside the time you already have. If you tell me you have no time but you watched a show last night, we have a different problem to solve and I can help with that too.
