What I see in this role

Two segments, two playbooks

R4527 splits across the Key Segment (1 to 3 providers) and the Strategic Segment (4 to 19 providers). The Key Segment buyer is a doctor owner with a lean budget who needs time back fast. The Strategic buyer weighs total cost of ownership and staff retention across multiple sites. Same funnel, different message. Below is a sample built for the Key Segment.

Sample campaign · Key Segment

Full funnel, mapped end to end

A 30 day campaign for 1 to 3 provider practices, themed "Get your evenings back."

MQLPaid social and email drive a "time back" guide download and webinar signups.
SALScore on practice size and role, then hand qualified leads to Sales Development.
SQLSDR books demos for doctor owners who fit the Key Segment profile.
PipelineTrack demo to opportunity, report weekly, and feed wins back into targeting.

Email

Subject tests: "Home by dinner, charts still done" and "The 6pm note problem." Short, plain, one ask.

Landing page

One promise, one form, one proof point. A 3 field form and a download that answers the top question doctors ask.

Paid social

Meta and LinkedIn to small practice owners. Test pain led versus outcome led creative, hold spend to the best CPL.
