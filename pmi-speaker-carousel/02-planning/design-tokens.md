# Design Tokens — PMISF-SPKR-2026

**Version:** 1.0 — supplied by the Sponsor from the official PMI brand guide (executes Decision D-4).

## Rule
Each design uses ONE palette family, monochromatic. Within a tangerine design, ONLY the six codes below may appear — no other hex values, no white (#FFFFFF; use Tangerine 50), no derived tints.
