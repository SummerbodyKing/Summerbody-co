**Methodology:** PMBOK — 5 Process Groups, 10 Knowledge Areas, 49 processes

## What we are building

A coded, animated **rotating speaker carousel** for PMI South Florida event promotion, where each speaker's headshot **pops out of a PMI-branded shape**, plus a **video export** for social media and a **reusable template system** so future speakers can be swapped in without redesigning.
